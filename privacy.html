Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 1, 2026

Overview

OpenCode Remote ("the App") is a companion client for the OpenCode AI coding assistant. It connects directly to a server that you own and operate. The App does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to the developer or any third party.

Data Collection

The App does not collect any user data. Specifically:

  • No analytics or tracking frameworks are included.
  • No crash reporting SDKs are included.
  • No advertising identifiers are used.
  • No personal information is sent to the developer or any third party.

Network Communication

All network traffic occurs exclusively between your device and your self-hosted OpenCode server. The App communicates over HTTP (local network) or HTTPS (public network) depending on your configuration. No data passes through any intermediary service controlled by the developer.

Local Storage

The App stores your server address, username, and connection preferences in local device storage (UserDefaults). Passwords and API tokens are stored in the iOS Keychain. SSH private keys, if generated, are also stored in the Keychain. None of this data leaves your device.

Microphone

The App may request microphone access for optional voice input. Audio is recorded locally and sent to a speech-to-text service that you configure yourself. The developer has no access to your audio recordings.

Changes

If this policy is updated, the new version will be posted at this URL.
